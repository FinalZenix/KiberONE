# GLOBAL CLASSROOM RULES

## Schedule & Timing
1. **Day:** Saturdays Only.
2. **Frequency:** Once per week per group.
3. **Structure:** 120 minutes total.
   - **Part 1:** 60 min (Theory + Practice)
   - **Break:** 10 min
   - **Part 2:** 50 min (Deep Work + Fun Activity)

## General Policies
1. **Internet Usage:** No internet for students in the first 30 minutes unless specifically required by the module.
2. **Breaks:** Students must leave the screen during the break.
3. **Language:** German is the primary language of instruction.
